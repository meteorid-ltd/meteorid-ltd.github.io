Our Mission

meteorid ltd is a USA-based Research & Development firm specializing in radiative transfer, machine learning, and atmospheric science. We are dedicated to translating theoretical and technological developments into tangible advancements across various sectors.

Our core mission is to engage in R&D activities, providing innovative solutions, products, and theoretical insights to our partners.

About the Founder

Dr. Milos Sztipanov is the founder and lead scientist of meteorid ltd. He is a physicist who specializes in radiative transfer, bringing a rigorous analytical approach to scientific inquiry. His expertise is in leveraging advanced computational tools—including Machine Learning, Python, FORTRAN, and high-performance computing—to translate abstract concepts into practical, innovative solutions.

In his recent role as a contract Physical Scientist at NOAA's National Weather Service (NCEP/EMC), Dr. Sztipanov worked on improving the radiation scheme in the Unified Forecast System (UFS) weather models. His work included formulating and developing a novel method for calculating atmospheric heating rates and pioneering research on a neural network to replace gas optics in the physical model.

Dr. Sztipanov earned his Ph.D. in physics from Stevens Institute of Technology, where his research focused on methods for retrieving ozone, cloud, and aerosol properties utilizing machine learning and ground-based measurements. He holds a Bachelor's Degree in physics with a theoretical physics concentration from Eötvös Lóránd Science University (HUN). He continues to contribute to the field as a Visiting Researcher at Stevens Institute of Technology and a Research Associate at the University of Maryland.

His research has been published in numerous peer-reviewed journals, including the Journal of Advances in Modeling Earth Systems, Atmosphere, Optics Express, and Applied Optics.
